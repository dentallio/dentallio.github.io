About Us

At dentall, we nurture each team member as a future leader through comprehensive guidance in management, leadership, and coaching.  Our dedication lies in establishing dentall's global influence and elevating the dental industry.

Sincere Communication

Embracing genuine and transparent communication, we encourage open expression of ideas and attentive listening to create a collaborative atmosphere. Together, we exchange ideas and propel towards our shared goals.

Resource Sharing

Embracing diversity and expertise, we promote a culture of collaboration and knowledge-sharing among team members. By leveraging each other's strengths, we enhance our overall competitiveness, unity, and achieve synchronized information flow.

Pursuit of Excellence

Aspiring to be industry leaders, we relentlessly seek breakthroughs and transcend the ordinary. With an unwavering commitment to excellence, we consistently push boundaries to achieve greater success.

Values

At dentall, we embody the principles of "solving problems, fulfilling needs, and creating value" to revolutionize the field of dentistry. In 2018, we embarked on our digital dentistry transformation journey with the establishment of the R&D department. With "dentallHiS", a cloud-based practice management system, we drive digital transformation, providing superior medical services for both dentists and patients.

Mission

In 2019, we took a major step towards global expansion by setting up a representative office in Vietnam to promote Taiwan's dental industries. During the same year, "dentallHiS" garnered attention at IDS Germany, the world's largest dental exhibition. In 2022, we established dentall's global corporate headquarter, introducing the concept of “dentaverse". In 2023, the integration of AI and dental expertise led us to unveil the groundbreaking product "dentall.ai," revolutionizing the industry and optimizing clinic efficiency.

Vision

Our vision is to create a global dental service platform, firmly embracing the idea of "leveraging technology to enhance oral health for everyone." We are committed to pursuing advancements in dentistry and technology, providing exceptional innovations and services to the global dental industry.

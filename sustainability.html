Sustainability in dentall

Starting with a mission to elevate public oral health through technology, dentall aims to become a pioneer in the dental industry by guiding dental practitioners in sustainable practices. The company focuses on governance, workers, community, customers, and the environment, striving for sustainability and aiming to benefit everyone.

Governance

dentall is making a statement about corporate sustainability. We aim to achieve everyone's agreement by using more comprehensive and efficient management approaches and by taking the initiative in sharing information with the public.

Introducing the company's corporate sustainability practices to the  employees during the orientation

Announcing a statement about sustainable practices in our company

Making a propaganda of anti-corruption to employees

Workers

dentall provides employee benefits such as external courses for career growth and conducts regular performance evaluations to ensure effective communication.

Offering free courses to assist employee for career growth

Monitoring the air quality and making improvements regularly to build a better working environment

Organizing the staff activities to promote inclusivity and foster unity among employees

Community

dentall actively participating in volunteer activities and collaborating with social welfare organizations to create a more compassionate and better society.

Creating the supplier code of conduct and sustainability

Taking the initiative to participate in dental volunteer service

Taking the initiative to contribute in community service and set the goal

Customers

dentall prioritizes customer satisfaction by continuously enhancing the quality of products and services and actively collecting customer feedback to resolve any issues.

Providing multi channels for customer’s service

Monitoring the customer’s satisfaction on product and service in regular basic

Application for information security certification to assure the safety of information

Environment

dentall is care for the environment, and aim to become the pioneer in Taiwan’s dental industry by leading the dental practitioners to create a better environment.

Our office is located in a certified green building

Publicizing the energy-saving to all employees

Collaborating with brands which are caring about the environment and strive for sustainability
